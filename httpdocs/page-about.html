We have about 14 Years of Diverse Experience

We are progressive global consulting and service providers who specialize in innovative technology and product development activities. Since 2008, we are committed to providing assured quality and services through onsite & offshore model of domestic and international clientele. We are teamed with a combination of highly diversified technocrats and functional experts who are passionate about providing complete Solutions towards sustained growth.

At Tecdatum, we understand the vision and business requirements of our clients and assist them to transform the same into reality as their Technology Partners. Our commitment and consistency at service delivery have built long term relationships with customers and partners globally.

We have grown from strength to strength and successfully completed many projects and have a large satisfied client base. With diversified services and broad Consulting, Geospatial Services and Software Development products & Services we are heading towards a futuristic arena of opportunities.

Our Values

Tecdatum is committed towards quality-of-service, on-schedule delivery and cost-effective solutions. Our work quotient is inclined towards, ‘Trust’, based on which we are connected to our satisfied clientele. Strategic thinking, excellent team, personal attention, competitive prices, process-driven approach, commitment, on time delivery, ensuring customer satisfaction is our USP.
